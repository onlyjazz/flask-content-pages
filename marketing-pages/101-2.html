Fanatic solutions for medtech
Run your Medtech studies in the cloud, not in a box of paper.

Clear Clinica provides practical solutions and  fanatical service for medtech vendors conducting clinical trials.

YOUR USERS SEND DATA
Your users send data to a Clear Clinica cloud app. A user can be a site coordinator/PI, patient at home or on ­the­ go, or a medical device reporting treatment and outcomes.
less
  • Global sites in your trial send data to a Clear Clinica cloud app
    • Site coordinators in a hospital or clinic use ECRF
    • Patients at home use a browser or their mobile devices to report outcomes using simple easy­-to­-use diaries.
    • Patients can use two-­way SMS to report treatment and compliance
    • Medical devices can send treatment and diagnostic data securely using a secure Restful API.
CLEAR CLINICA RECEIVES THE DATA
The Clear Clinica cloud platform receives the data and updates a secure central database in real time.
more
  • Your data is secure and always accessible
    • Stay in touch with the progress of the study wherever you are
    • No export/import. No third-party analytics required. No risk of downloaded patient / clinical trial data files being stolen by malware.
MONITORING LOGIC TAKES OVER
Your cloud app executes edit checks and updates your user in real time. Clear Clinica Alerts and Analytics pinpoint issues and visualize trends for you and your CRO.
more
  • Easy-to-use cloud Monitoring as a Service technology provides a real-time picture of all sites and subjects and enables you to prioritize site visits to resolve quality and protocol compliance issues. Real time alerts and analytics enable you to:
    • Analyse and track the progress of the study recruitment, adverse events, protocol compliance and more with easy-to-use and powerful Risk App dashboards
    • Stay in touch with the progress of the study wherever you are: Alerts and analytics are accessible in your browser or mobile device.
    • No export/import, third-party analytics required. No security exposure of data downloaded to a user PC for processing in Excel,
    • Multiple access to alerts and analytics enables the entire team to be on the same page and share action items.
