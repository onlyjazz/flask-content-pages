VISION

Clear Clinica is an Israeli hi-tech startup based in Modiin, Israel that develops and delivers the Managed Clinical Cloud: an innovative cloud service designed especially for innovative Israeli biotech and medical device sponsors who want to reduce their time to market, slash their costs for global multi-center clinical trial operations and ensure regulatory compliance.
Our multi-disciplinary team brings diverse backgrounds in software, security, data science and medical science to bring high-value and low-cost clinical data management and clinical data monitoring cloud services to Israeli life science companies.
For more information contact us today.
CLEAR CLINICA - HIGH VALUE, LOW COST
The Managed Clinical Cloud seamlessly integrates online clinical data capture (also known as EDC or ECRF) with online clinical data monitoring that replaces costly manual people processes and eliminate avoidable rework.

Clear Clinica Monitoring as a Service was created to meet the highest regulatory, information technology and data security requirements for online clinical data collection and clinical data monitoring at a price that is 30-50% than any competing solution for risk-based monitoring that is currently available to the Israeli clinical trial industry today.
The Managed Clinical Cloud is a secure cloud-based service that enables sponsors to quickly implement a world-class solution for online clinical data capture that combines remote clinical data monitoring with advanced analytics and dashboards in order to pinpoint issues with clinical data quality and patient safety at a fraction of the cost of any competing solution currently available to the Israeli clinical trial industry.
Clear Clinica Monitoring as a Service is a secure cloud-based service that enables you to search and visualize your time-oriented clinical data in order to monitor your data and your patients’ safety in real time without the complexity and cost of data mining software and without the exposure to data leaks when you download patient data to your PC for analysis.
The Managed Clinical Cloud is an ideal platform for Israeli biotech and biomed sponsors who have a limited budget and want to implement remote risk-based monitoring according to FDA Guidance for Industry – Oversight of Clinical Investigations — A Risk-Based Approach to Monitoring
