PARTNERS

We partner with industry leading companies to fully meet your needs and solve the challenges of global multi-center clinical trials operations with solutions that have unprecedented cost-effectiveness.

The Israeli medtech industry is special not only in its ground-breaking innovation but in not having a domestic market for its products. Without a domestic market, Israeli medtech must innovate locally and work globally in their clinical research, manufacturing, sales and marketing.

Clear Clinica works with global partners to unleash the potential of the cloud for thinking locally and working globally.

Zifo RnD

When we first set out on the path of revolutionizing clinical study monitoring, we knew we could not go it alone.

Zifo RnD, based in Chennai is our clinical study implementation partner. Zifo is a perfect fit for us, as they thrive on challenges with ambitious Research Organizations. Whether they are in Pharmaceuticals, Biotech, Medical devices or in CRO space, through their deep understanding of the research domain and with their technological expertise, Zifo helps us overcome the toughest challenges of the customers of the Managed Clinical Cloud.

Research for you

Are you a patient interested in learning more about how innovative Israeli life science research can help you or a family member?
Research for you is a young dynamic Israeli initiative that helps patients find cutting edge clinical trials and helps Israeli life science developers accelerate their patient recruitment process using the Internet.
Research for you offers, free-of-charge to patients, a highly effective online platform that supports both demand and supply-side of qualified patients for clinical trials in Israel.
On the demand side, Research for you provides a friendly and convenient way for prospective subjects to discover clinical trials in Israel that may help them.
On the supply side,Research for you provides leads of potential patients to sponsors by presenting and nurturing an online presence for a clinical trial that communicates information to potential subjects about the trial, the team, its advantages and criteria and allow them to express an interest to join.
